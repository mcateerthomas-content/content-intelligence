The data answers whether members are consuming what the content team produces. They are — and it keeps them.

What's working and what comes next
01
Benchmarks and surveys lead in per-piece engagement (40.9). The "What's Working in AI" cross-network benchmark is the next major initiative: 15–20 question survey, 20 cross-cluster interviews, artifact compilation. Closes May 1, 2026.
02
Downloadable content is the fastest-growing format (+338% YoY) and is drawing new users at nearly 3x the rate (+294% YoY). The content strategy should continue shifting production capacity toward downloadable, argued formats.
03
Communities of Interest — the New to Role Exchange program launches April 17 with eight monthly sessions through December. These are cross-network content moments that serve both member engagement and prospect awareness.
04
The production ratio — argued content at 22.8 vs. readouts at 10.2 — suggests the organization should invert its current 1:3 ratio of argued-to-summary content. Every piece of argued content that enters the archive serves both retention and pipeline.

Three independent data sets point the same direction: content drives acquisition, engagement, and retention.
